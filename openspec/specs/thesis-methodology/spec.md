## Purpose

This capability defines the thesis methodology sections (Problemática, Justificación, Objetivos) that form the introductory content of the SRS document, establishing the scientific motivation and objectives for the SGR system.

## Requirements

### Requirement: Thesis problem statement (Problemática)
The SRS document SHALL include a "Problemática" subsection in the Introducción that details the fundamental problems in traditional requirements engineering that motivate the thesis.

#### Scenario: Problemática subsection present
- **WHEN** the Introducción section is reviewed
- **THEN** it SHALL include subsection 1.1 Problemática describing ambiguity in traditional requirements elicitation, information loss in stakeholder interviews, and lack of formal structure in software requirements documentation

### Requirement: Thesis justification (Justificación)
The SRS document SHALL include a "Justificación" subsection that establishes the scientific rationale for using Large Language Models and Whisper transcription as the solution approach.

#### Scenario: Justificación subsection present
- **WHEN** the Introducción section is reviewed
- **THEN** it SHALL include subsection 1.2 Justificación detailing why LLMs and automated transcription constitute a scientifically grounded response to the identified problems

### Requirement: Thesis objectives (Objetivos)
The SRS document SHALL include an "Objetivos" subsection with one general objective and multiple specific objectives oriented to the SGR system.

#### Scenario: Objetivos subsection present
- **WHEN** the Introducción section is reviewed
- **THEN** it SHALL include subsection 1.3 Objetivos containing one general objective and 4-6 specific objectives aligned with the SGR capabilities
